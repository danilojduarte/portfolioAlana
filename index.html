ABOUT ME

Design has
been my passion
since I was barely
old enough to
sharpen my own
pencils.

I am a Natal City, Brazil native and love the beauty of my home city. As a child I spent most of my time drawing and creating works of art. That passion has carried through my life in so many ways.

My passion turned into a career focus as I began winning design contests on the competitive site 99Designs. I began designing as full time freelancer in 2012 and now I have a wide range of experience with clients from all over the world including Canada and the United States.

My hard work and creativity has served me well and I have been busy freelancing ever since. I currently work as a partner at WellPut Custom Content and Design and continue to freelance for clients locally and internationally.
